Education
Resonite can be used in the classroom environment. Anyone can create custom teaching module experiences. Experience the surface of other celestial bodies through 3D scanned imagery, learn to identify coral reef health, maybe even teach a course in visual scripting via Protoflux.

Live Production

Resonite’s technology makes it not just easy to stream live content, but also gives the ability to create versatile and interactive productions. Tweak your stage on the fly, have a team off camera control lighting and cameras, pipe in audience chat commands to trigger events in your session. The possibilities are endless!
